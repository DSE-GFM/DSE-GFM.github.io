Introduction

Greetings! We are researchers from the Data Science and Engineering (DSE) Laboratory at Michigan State University. Currently, we are exploring the possibility of Graph Foundation Models (GFM), with collaborators from both academia and industry. Our research comprehensively spans various dimensions of GFM, including its principles, architecture, data management, and the design of pretext tasks. It is our pleasure to share our latest advancements with you through this website. Here, you will find an array of resources such as our published papers, presentations, and information about workshops dedicated to GFM. Moreover, we are keenly open to exploring potential collaborations. Let's work towards GFM together!
